When I first found out that Ultra Q would be coming to Switzerland I became ecstatic. I've been a fan since 2021 and couldnt stop listening to them. They had two shows in Switzerland. One in Aarau and the other in Winterthur. I would've loved to have gone to both but I had a Physics exam on the following day after the Aarau concert. Anyways, i kinda forced one of my friend to come with. Luckily, she agreed. I dont know anyone that has a very similar music taste than me in the real world. Its always hard finding people to come with me to these shows. Anyways, on the friday night of the Winterthur concert we ended up arriving an hour earlier than expected. The concert app I was using showed us the wrong time when the doors would be open. So there was me and my friend out in the freezing cold and to top it off, it was snowing.
